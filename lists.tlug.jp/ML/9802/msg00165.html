JPNIC requires 20,000 yen for one *.jp domain at the registration.
They don't need annual fee for that domain name.  The registered
domain should be connected in one year from registration.

http://www.nic.ad.jp/jpnic/
http://www.nic.ad.jp/jpnic/faq/QandA.html#4010
ftp://ftp.nic.ad.jp/jpnic/domain/domain-name-all.txt
ftp://ftp.nic.ad.jp/jpnic/domain/domain-name-fee.txt

All sources up above are in Japanese.
