Hi all,

     I have got to make this announced clearly.

     As some people already know I've obtained a HP PostScript Printer
LaserJet4000N.
I came across a pathetic touble while printing. The problem under win95
is,(perhaps
under NT as well),

1) As soon as you want to print out something from Netscape related
apps. such as
   Communicator, mailer, or Compser, the whole system freezed in terms
of printing.
   No application can print anything.

2) There have been some advises that IE and Netscape are not good
friends so I got
   rid of IE completely caz I hate this funny interface.

3) Printing went all right. Or seemed to be Okay.

4) After rebooting, no way. Again the system freezes.

5) Erros are usually at modules;

   gdi.exe
   kln386.exe(only on an old machines).


   I checked every single manual, aksed people, sent emails,
buuuuuuuuuuuuuuut
no one actually could answer me. Some pals recommended me to change the
printer.
Or just throw the printer out the window and make a mess of the
sidewalk. hahhaa

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The final solution.

     I visited last night with HP headquarter at California and talked
to a printer
support tech over the phone for over ten minutes. Hahahaha expensive way
to crack
it!!!

     Anyway, solution is as follows.

1) edit win.ini
   add c:\windows\system\win87em.exe

2) shutdown the system and reboot but only to dos prompt.

3) cd c:\windows\system
   ren win87em.dll win87em.exe

4) reboot the system again.

That is it folks. so simple. buuuuuuuuuuuut this was the final way to
cope with
the problem. The support tech himself tried a few things. and finally
came to
this. hahahahhaha He said 'Sir, this is Microsoft. They want to kill
Netscape.
wahahahhahahaha


Yours.
Yong-Ming Hua
