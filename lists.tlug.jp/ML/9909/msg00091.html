  TLUG - Tokyo Linux Users Group "nomikai" Meeting 9/17
  =====================================================

    *** Linux 8th Birthday Anniversary Party !! ***

Date:     September 17, 1999 (Friday)

Time:     19:30 

Place:    Tengu TokyoEkiMae
          Chuo-ku, Kyobashi 1-1-6, EchiZenYa Bld. B1/B2
          Phone: 03-3275-3691

Event:    drink and eat (primarily a social activity)

Costs:    will be divided up among participants
          see http://tlug.gr.jp/meetings/index.html 
          for information on costs of previous events

This event is open to all Linux users and friends, and is
to promote casual Linux (and non-Linux) related discussions
over a few cold ones. Even if you're not a Linux user or a
LUG member, but want to hang out with Linux people, please come.

September 17 is a very special date, as the first public release
of Linux (v0.01) occurred on the 17th of September 1991. 

           *** It's Linux birthday anniversary! ***
   http://www.vision25.demon.co.uk/prog/linuxbirthday.html

Let's celebrate it together as Linux turns 8 years old.

If you're coming, send an e-mail to tlug-admin@example.com
just to let us know so that we can reserve space at Tengu. If you
want to come at the last minute and don't send an e-mail, it's 
okay - we may have to squeeze a bit, but please come!

Tengu is about 5 minutes away from the Yaesu Chuo exit of the JR Tokyo
train station.  It is on the right hand side of Yaesu Dori if you are
walking out of the station.  If you are walking out of Yaesu exit, turn
the map upside down.

If you are coming via the underground mall, look for the Starbucks
across from McDonalds, and take the next exit on the left up to ground
level. If you get lost, or simply want to get in touch with us, please call:

   Alberto Tomita......070-5644-3680 (PHS)
   Shigeo Honda........090-4396-0445 (NTT DoCoMo)
   Jonathan Byrne......070-5517-9250 (PHS)

For more information, access the TLUG web page at http://www.tlug.gr.jp

See you there!

        T L U G 「Linux 8歳の誕舌逅跂闕鮟砲Σ顱
日時：　9月17日金曜日、19時30分

場所：　居酒屋「天狗」東京駅前店
　　　　東京都中央区京橋 1-1-6、越前屋ビル 地下１階
　　　　電話番号：(03) 3275-3691

参加費：飲食代金は基本的に参加者で割り勘にします。
       過去の費用実舌逅跂闕を目安にしてください。
      （http://tlug.gr.jp/meetings/index.html 参照）

場所は東京駅、八重洲中央口から歩いて約５分の、「天狗」東京駅前店
で、時間は１９：３０からになります。会場ではLinuxの話題に花を咲か
せつつ、ビールやサングリア、ワインなどで楽しく過ごします。Linux
ノートPCを持ち込んでの自慢大会や即舌逅跂闕LANでのデータ交換なども可能です。
Linux userでないお友達もごいっしょに。

今回の飲み会は 9月17日、Linux の誕舌逅跂闕世修Δ任
頒兎就蔗痕蜩蜿邁貴粤迴隨竢覩頏閾蜴砠鶯葢癨譬乗雕皷闔乙纃闔鎬鳫膀跚銛蜥籬蔗迪鹿曽
ので、皆と一緒にお祝いしましょう！

ご出舌逅跂闕縦蠅諒粱蜴舌逅跂闕へメールください。日本語／英語
どちらでもOKです。

当日、道がわからなかったり、質問がある場合は下記の電話番号ま
でどうぞ。
