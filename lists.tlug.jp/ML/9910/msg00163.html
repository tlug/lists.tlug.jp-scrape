It's a call for participation in the Linux Community BOF session
to be held during the Linux Conference 99.

The conference will happen on 12/17-18 and the BOF will be on
the second day, Saturday, around 2PM. In order to participate,
pre-registration is required: http://lc99.linux.or.jp/bof.html 

BTW, tomorrow is the last day for pre-registration at discounted 
rates for the conference. More details at http://lc99.linux.or.jp
