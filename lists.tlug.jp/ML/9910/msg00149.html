Hallo to everybody from Munich!
I am still alive and I have finally setteled into my new apartment
after a week of cleaning everything to a livable state (especially
 the kitchen). University starts next week,

An IT expo called SYSTEMS'99 (supposed to be the second biggest
in Europe after CeBIT ) had been going on and they had a special
Linux section called Linux Park. And guess.. I was there for 3 weeks
representing Debain at a booth the German Linux-Magazin  sponsored.

Some people asked about CJK on Linux and I directed them to TLUG.
Also was quite interesting to learn about German LUGs . There will
be a "LUG-DE Camp" next June where 150 - 200 Linuxers will gather
on a (former) farm to camp and Linux for the whole weekend. I will
definetly go.

BTW, photos and an small summary (in ja_JP) of SYSTEMS will appear shortly
at http://www.linuxjapan.com/ljexpress/ . Tux @ Microsoft was real fun.
Other Debian guys and me went around carring my 1m Tux and when we passed
the MS booth,the CD-give-away girl all came to Tux ("How cuuuuuuteeeeee").
When I wanted to take a picture, a guy in a suit came running, yelling
at as "No photos!". and chased us away :-)
