I must admit to a primitive system for viewing Japanese email, but I don't get
much. I use a mail client (xfmail) that has no provision for double-byte
characters, so when Japanese email comes in I click on ``external viewer,''
which spawns leim-enabled emacs 20.4 with my message in it.
Sometimes this works but sometimes I can't view the text at
all. Here's an example of text I can't read (i.e., it appears as dollar signs,
punctuation, letters and numbers):
