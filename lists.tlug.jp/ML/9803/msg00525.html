Though this is not related to the original subject, I found in
the Outlook setting at my office that you can make the above
Japanese to English words by setting:

Tool (ツール) (T) ->
Option (オプション) (O) ->
開封 tab ->
Check 「返信／転送時に英語のヘッダーを使用する(U)」 on

I hope this helps.
