TOKYO LINUX USERS GROUPの４月ミーティングのご案内をいたします。

今回はNetscape社のInternational Components Groupの技術リーダー
Tague Griffith氏を迎え、Netscapeのオープンソースコードと
CommunicatorのInternationalizationについての講演があります。

Linux愛好者、オープンソースコード/フリーソフトウエアの
サポーターの方どなたでもご参加ください。参加の条件は特にあり

を集める場合があります。

ツールに関する情報交換のための非営利の任意団体です。
TLUGはメーリングリスト、ホームページ、定期的なミーティングを
通して活発に活動しています。議論／情報交換はおもに英語で行な
われていますが、どなたでも歓迎です。特に日本人／日本語を話す
方の参加によってバイリンガルなミーティングを推進することを希
望しています。

今回の特別テーマ

  **** Learn Netscape's Open Source ****

Netscape Communications社のInternational Components groupの
テクニカルリーダであるTague Griffith氏による、Communicator
のソースコードリリースについての講演です。International
Components groupはNetscapeのクライアントおよびサーバー製品
向けのクロスプラットフォームinternationalizationプラット
フォームを提供しています。

Griffith氏は以前Apple ComputerでText Services Manager
input methodアーキテクチャの開発を担当されました。氏はまた、
札幌のビーユージーでの勤務経験もあります。

ミーティングに参加できない方でGriffith氏に質問がある方は、
下記のフォームを使って質問をお送りください。
http://beehive.twics.com/~tlug/meeting.html. 
ミーティング後にTLUGのホームページに回答を掲載する予定です。


 **** Demonstration of Grabber Driver and Application

TLUGのアクティブメンバーであるMarcus Metzler氏と彼のお兄さん
(弟かも)によるグラバードライバーとLinuxアプリケーションのデモ
です。彼らのソフトウエアはBt848ベースのビデオグラバーカードを
使ってX11上に画像を表示します。詳しくは下記のページをごらんく
ださい。
	http://www.thp.Uni-Koeln.DE/~rjkm/linux/bttv.html

		       Open TLUG April 11 Meeting
		       --------------------------

		    All Open Source Groups Welcome
		    ------------------------------

Featuring Netscape's technical leader of their International 
Components Group, Tague Griffith, Speaking on the Open Source 
Code for Netscape and internationalization Efforts for Communicator


All Linux lovers and supporters of open source code
and free software are invited to attend
the April 11 Tokyo Linux Users Group meeting.  Membership
is open to anyone.  There are currently no membership dues.

TLUG is a non-profit, non-political organization established 
in June of 1994 to exchange information on the use of Linux 
and Free Software Foundation tools.  TLUG has an active
mailing list, home page, and regular physical meetings.  
Discussions are primarily held in English.  However, 
everyone is welcome to attend.  We especially want to encourage
Japanese people to attend and will make efforts to 
support a bilingual meeting.  

The TLUG home page is available at
	http://www.twics.com/~tlug/


Pictures and notes of the February 14 meeting, including pictures
are at:
	http://www.twics.com/~tlug/9802/index.html


Notes of the January 14th drinking party, including pictures of some
of the members are available at
	http://www.twics.com/~tlug/9801/index.html

Lunch Meeting Time: 12:30
Lunch Meeting Place: Tokyo JR Station Yaesu Chuo exit
Lunch Eating Place: Tai Kou Rou  03-3271-9351  map below

Meeting Time: 13:45 or 14:00 (after we finish eating)

Most of the benefit of the meeting comes 
from chatting with other Linux users and having informal 
discussions.  Lunch is one of the main reasons for going 
to the meetings for many people.  Everyone is expected 
to pay for their own meal.

    ** Make Friends **


We will be meeting at Tokyo station on April 11th, Saturday
in front of the Tokyo station Yaesu central ticket gate at 12:30.
There is more than one Yaesu ticket gate, so be careful.  If you
know you are going and don't know what we look like, send
me an e-mail (craig@example.com) and I'll make sure we wait 
for you.  After everyone gathers, we'll walk down the stairs 
and have lunch in the restaurant area in the basement of 
Tokyo station.  If you're late or can't find us, 
go directly to Joe Marchak's office.  

  **** Learn Netscape's Open Source **** 

Meeting Topic: Tague Griffith of Netscape i18n will be talking about the
release of the Communicator source code. He is currently employed at
Netscape Communications, where he is the technical lead for the
International Components group. The International Components group
supplies cross-platform internationalization libraries to Netscape's client
and server products. 

Previosuly, Tague worked for Apple Computer on the Text Services
Manager input method architecture. He has also worked for B.U.G. in
Sapporo, Japan. 

Tague graduated from Stanford University with a bachelor's degree in
computer science. In his free time, Tague does free lance writing and is
involved in the San Francisco music scene. 

If you will be unable to attend this meeting but have a question for Mr.
Griffith, please use the form found at
http://beehive.twics.com/~tlug/meeting.html. 
Answers to submitted questions will be posted to the TLUG homepage 
after the meeting. 


**** Demonstration of Grabber Driver and Application

Marcus Metzler and his brother will demonstrate the grabber driver
and application they wrote for Linux.
The software enables you to use TV grabber cards
based on the Bt848 chip to watch TV on your X11 screen. It supports 
various hardware cards by several manufacturers. For more information
see:  http://www.thp.Uni-Koeln.DE/~rjkm/linux/bttv.html
