If you don't want to get hassled, I would suggest avoid Fry's
Electronics and T-Zone... Most of the stores I know of are in the
Silicon Valley... if I don't have to go to Fry's, then there's NCA...
the one I normally go to is located next to Price-Costco off of Lawrence
Expressway... that was also the first store that I learned about
Linux... and they were selling Ygradrassil Linux over there... not sure
if they still sell that there though since I haven't been there for
about 4 years now... 

Otherwise, just pick up a copy of MicroTimes or Computer Currents and
take a look at the advertisements (you can find these magazines for free
in front of Fry's... apparently the only good thing that Fry's is for)
