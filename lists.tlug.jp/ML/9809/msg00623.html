Two things come to mind:

 - "wget", which is a program/routine for eaching out & grabbing WW pages.
Pretty high-powered. In C I think.

 - webget. Jeffrey Friedl's perl program to do much the same thing. You
can get it from his WWW page (http://enterprise.ic.gc.ca/~jfriedl/) or I
can email it to you. I use this in the URL-gloss function in WWWJIC.
