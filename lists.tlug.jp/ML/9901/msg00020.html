                 TLUG January NOMIKAI
                 ====================

*** This month the TLUG Nomikai meeting will be held on a Thursday,
*** as Friday Jan 15 is a national holiday in Japan.

Date:     January 14th, 1999 (*Thursday*)
Time:     19:30 
Place:    Tengu TokyoEkiMae
          Chuo-ku, Kyobashi 1-1-6, EchiZenYa Bld. B1/B2
          Phone: 03-3275-3691
Events:   drink and eat (primarily a social activity)
Costs:    will be divided up among participants, see
          http://tlug.linux.or.jp/meetings/index.html for information
          on costs of previous events

Hi Everyone,

The 11th TLUG Nomikai is on January 14th, at Tokyo Station.
This event is open to all Linux users and friends, and is
to promote casual Linux (and non-Linux) related discussions
over a few cold ones.

Even if you're not a Linux user or a TLUG member, but want to hang out
with Linux people, please come. We have reservations for 25 people, but
more can squeeze in.

If you don't know where Tengu is, someone will be waiting at Tokyo station
Yaesu chuo ticket gate at 19:15.  Here are some pictures of Yaesu Chuo ticket
gate:
   http://tlug.linux.or.jp/meetings/9712/tldec00.jpg
   http://www.imasy.or.jp/~tam/off/TLUG01/jpg/r0100065.jpg

If you're coming, send an e-mail to tlug-admin@example.com just to
let us know so that we can reserve space at Tengu.  If you want to come
at the last minute and don't send an e-mail, it's okay - we may have to
squeeze a bit, but please come!

Tengu is about 5 minutes away from the Yaesu Chuo exit of the JR Tokyo
train station.  It is on the right hand side of Yaesu Dori if you are
walking out of the station.  If you are walking out of Yaesu exit, turn
the map upside down.

If you are coming via the underground mall, look for the Starbucks
across from McDonalds, and take the next exit on the left up to ground
level.

This is a picture of the Tengu sign in front of the Tengu we are going to.
   http://www.imasy.or.jp/~tam/off/TLUG01/jpg/r0100070.jpg

If you have a digital camera, please bring it and take  pictures.  We need
a photographer for the web page.

See you there!

Mobile Contacts:
   If you need to get in touch with us, please call.

   Alberto Tomita......070-5644-3680 (PHS)
   Shigeo Honda........090-4396-0445 (NTT DoCoMo)
   Jonathan Byrne......070-5517-9250 (PHS)

TLUG飲み会のご案内をさせていただきます。

Tokyo Linux Users Groupの今月の集会は、１月１４日の「飲み会」
です。

場所は東京駅、八重洲中央口から歩いて約５分の、「天狗」東京駅前店
で、時間は１９：３０からになります。

会場ではLinuxの話題に花を咲かせつつ、ビールやサングリア、ワイン
特製カクテルなどで楽しく過ごします。Linux Note PC(Desktopも可)を
持ち込んでの自慢大会や即舌逅跂闕LANでのデータ交換なども可能です。

会費は特に決めていません。解散時の勘定を割勘します。ご存知の
とおり、天狗のことですので頑張って飲んでもそんなに高くはならない
と思います。過去の実舌逅跂闕は１人あたり2,500-3,500円くらいだったと
記憶しています。

TLUGの活動、過去の集会の様子はhttp://tlug.linux.or.jp/をごらん
ください。

東京近郊のLinux愛好家の皆様、当日東京出張の方々、ぜひどうぞ
TLUG Nomikaiにご参加ください。

ご質問がありましたら、tlug-admin@example.com または 
atomita@example.com へメールください。日本語／英語どちらでもOKです。
