壽緕蒹蜚蓍鱇艨釶ぢあ appears in the lower left
hand side of the screen.  To switch to hiragana, I then
type ESC C-k while I'm in the fence.  Then I get the 
little katakana a ア.  At this point I can type 
クレッグ.  However, to get to the ten, I have to press
return, then start another fence, then switch to hiragana
with ESC C-h, then type in ten to get the dot ・瘢雹.  After 
I switch to hiragana, my last name 鈿鳫碎纃

in Japanese text.  For example, is it better to use.

こにちは弔任后跚銛諷鴈繻ぢについて知りたいこと
がありますが。

or

こにちは弔任后譯蕋遑。襭紕鬘遑紕譟。押ィ亜ィ横
ぢについて知りたいことがありますが。

would prefer to use hankaku since it takes up less space.
